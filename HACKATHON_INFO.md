# ET Gen AI Hackathon Information

This document serves as a reference for all the rules, guidelines, deadlines, and submission requirements for the **ET Gen AI Hackathon**, hosted by The Economic Times and Unstop.

---

## Event Overview
*   **Event:** India's Largest GenAI Hackathon
*   **Total Prize Pool:** ₹10 Lakh 
    *   1st Place: ₹5,00,000 + Incubation/Job Offer
    *   2nd Place: ₹3,00,000 + Mentorship
    *   3rd Place: ₹2,00,000 + Mentorship
*   **Participants:** Designed for students, professionals, and researchers.

## Important Dates
*   **Dec 26:** Registrations Open (Team Registration and Idea Submission)
*   **Mar 16:** Shortlisting and Problem Statement Release
*   **Mar 29:** Prototype Submission Deadline (Ends March 29th, 2026)
*   **TBA:** Presentations & Winners Announced

---

## Submission Checklist (Phase 2 & 3)
To successfully submit the project, the following assets must be created and publicly accessible:

1.  **Idea PPT:** Presentation of the problem statement, solution approach, and GenAI innovation.
2.  **Public GitHub Repository:** Complete source code, proper structure, comments, and commit history.
3.  **Architecture Diagram:** High-level diagram showing components, data flow, and GenAI integration.
4.  **Readme Documentation:** Clear setup instructions, usage guide, tech stack, and solution approach.
5.  **Functional Prototype:** Working demo of the GenAI solution with core features implemented.
6.  **Demo Video:** Walkthrough video showcasing the solution, features, and capabilities.

---

## Judging Criteria (The 5 Pillars)
Projects will be evaluated by industry experts based equally on these 5 factors:

*   **20% Innovation And Creativity:** Is the solution original? Does it offer a fresh perspective using GenAI?
*   **20% Technical Implementation:** Code quality, effective use of GenAI technologies, scalability, and technical soundness.
*   **20% Practical Impact:** Does it solve a real-world problem? What is the potential impact?
*   **20% User Experience:** Ease of use, interface design, accessibility, and overall UX.
*   **20% Pitch Quality:** Clarity of presentation, demo quality, and communication of the value proposition.

---

## Rules and Guidelines
*   **Original Work:** All ideas, code, documents, and assets MUST be original and created during the hackathon. Plagiarism or use of pre-built projects leads to immediate disqualification.
*   **Team Composition:** 1-4 members. No changes allowed after the Phase 1 deadline.
*   **Tools & Resources:** Open-source tools, libraries, and models are allowed. Licensed tools/datasets require valid authorization.
*   **Public Access:** All submission links (GitHub, Demo Videos, Documents) must remain public and accessible.
*   **Submission Platform:** All submissions must be made exclusively through the Unstop platform.
